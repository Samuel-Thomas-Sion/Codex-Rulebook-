📜 PROTECTORS OF THE CODEX — OFFICIAL RULEBOOK
I. CORE PRINCIPLE
This club is built on three pillars:
• Activity
• Progression
• Respect

Every member must contribute to at least one.
II. MEMBERSHIP EXPECTATIONS
• Remain active regularly
• Participate in at least one activity
• Maintain respectful communication

Inactivity may lead to removal.
III. ACTIVITY REQUIREMENT
Active means:
• Club games OR
• Chat participation OR
• Event participation

Minimum: 1 action every 3–5 days
IV. RANKING SYSTEM
Initiate → Keeper → Guardian → Warden → Grand Protector

Promotions are earned through activity, contribution, and trust.
V. RECRUITMENT SYSTEM
• 5 successful invites → Admin (Provisional)
• Must maintain activity
• Spam invites ignored
VI. AUTHORITY & ROLES
• Super Admin — Restricted control
• Admin — Events & moderation
• Coordinator — Engagement support

Authority is based on trust.
VII. EVENTS & MATCHES
• Daily chess matches
• Tournament expansion system

Members are expected to participate.
VIII. CODE OF CONDUCT
No toxicity, spam, cheating, or abuse.
Violations may lead to removal.
IX. ACTIVITY SYSTEM
Tracks:
• Participation
• Contribution
• Consistency

Rewards:
• Rank promotion
• Recognition
• Leadership opportunities
